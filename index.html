0xStackTooDeep Hackathon 2023

0xStackTooDeep is a 3 days long hackathon organized By BlocSoc(Sponsored Py GWEI & WEI) with the goal of inspiring and fostering a community of Builders promoting technological advancements in the Web3 space with a special focus on Web3 security. Packed with amazing sessions By industry eaperts and workshops, we invite you to grab the opportunity to interact and learn from the leading minds in the ecosystem and Build secure and innovative decentralized applications for the future.
